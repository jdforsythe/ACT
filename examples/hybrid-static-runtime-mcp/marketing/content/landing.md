Welcome to Acme. We help product teams ship agent-friendly websites by exposing
their content as ACT (Agent Content Tree) — a stable, machine-discoverable
shape that LLMs and crawlers can consume without bespoke integrations.

This page is the marketing landing node served by the static `/marketing`
mount of the PRD-706 hybrid example. The same tree is reachable through the
ACT-MCP bridge at `act://acme.local/marketing/landing`, byte-identical to
the HTTP fetch under PRD-706-R13's drift-prevention rule.
