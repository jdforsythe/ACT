We held the May offsite at a barn outside Stockholm. Three days,
twelve people, the goal being a shared picture of what we want the
team to look like at the end of 2026.

## Shape decisions

The infra team grows by two more (one SRE, one platform). The
product engineering team stays at five — the constraint is design
review capacity, not engineering. We add a customer engineering role
that did not previously exist; the role's remit is migration
planning and customer-side post-incident review.

## Process decisions

The on-call rotation moves from weekly to four-day shifts so anyone
can take a long weekend without trading. The release cadence stays
weekly but the cut-time shifts from Friday to Tuesday so any release
issues land on a workday.
