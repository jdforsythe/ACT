The community Discord generated a lot of questions in the run-up to
GA. We collected the top fifteen and answered them here so customers
who are not on Discord get the same view.

## Pricing

Yes, the new pricing applies to private beta accounts at their
renewal. No, there is no grandfathering for the legacy per-request
egress fee — the fee is going away across the board.

## Support

Yes, the GA support tier includes a 24-hour first-response SLA on
P2 and above. The free tier has community support only; the
business tier adds 12-hour first-response on P1 and above.
