We are launching Tinybox today. After eighteen months of building,
the public beta is open and the waitlist closes for new accounts in
six weeks.

## What it is

Tinybox is a storage primitive for teams that want object storage
semantics without operating an object store. It runs on a managed
control plane with a regional data plane the customer can pin or
let us pick.

## Why it exists

The existing options skew either toward heavyweight self-hosted
clusters or toward cloud-vendor-locked pricing. Tinybox is the
middle ground: managed control plane, portable data plane, no
per-request egress fees under 10 TB.
