The private beta wrapped on April 18. Six months, 217 accounts, 4.8
PB stored at peak, 14 production-grade incidents. The recap is
public; the per-account details are not.

## What we learned about the product

The customers who stayed past the second month were the ones whose
existing storage workflows were already CLI-driven. Customers whose
workflows started in a UI churned at twice the rate, even when the
underlying use case was identical.

## What we learned about the team

The on-call rotation we designed for ten people did not scale to
fifteen without re-thinking the handoff. The change is small but
worth documenting for the next time we grow the team.
