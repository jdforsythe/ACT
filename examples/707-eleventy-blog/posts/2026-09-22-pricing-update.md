Today we are publishing the fall 2026 pricing update. The changes
land on October 1; existing accounts continue to bill at their
current rate through the end of their current billing cycle.

## What's changing

A new starter tier ships at $9 per month for accounts under 100 GB.
Storage costs across all tiers drop by 18%. Per-request egress fees
disappear for any account under 10 TB; egress within the same region
remains free at every tier.

## Rationale

The storage market hit our internal break-even target two quarters
ahead of plan. Pushing the savings to customers is a more sustainable
growth lever than reinvesting in marketing.
